WHAT WE VALUE

TEAMWORK

Here at Horizons we value working as a team and aspiring to achieve goals as a unit. Although we love individual success, achieving milestones as a unit is priceless and desirable here at Horizons.

HONESTY

Horizons holds accountability and honesty to a high standard for both the company as a whole, and our individual team members. We wish to be open with our members, team and audience.

TRANSPERANCY

Transparency goes hand in hand with honesty, as allowing your audience and members access to what goes on in the company is crucial for loyalty, understanding, and fairness.

ADAPTABILITY

Being able to adapt to different technologies and enviornments is crucial for success, not only to a business but you as an individual. And we are an adaptable bunch here at Horizons.

RESPECT

Without respect, there is no love. Respect is a core part of Horizons, as we value not only your skills but who you are as a human being. We value each individual in our community and treat everyone with the same respect.

FUN

We know work can be challenging and we all need to lock in, however this doesn't mean work should not be fun and enjoyable. Above all, we want to enjoy our journey with a smile on our face, and that is what we do here at Horizons.

QUALITY

Quality over quanity is a motto we go by. Through our design, communication, art, service etc, we put strong emphasis in creating quality service and content over flooding the market with mediocrity.

BALANCE

No matter how much you are making, no money is worth having your life imprisoned by your occupation. This is why we value having a work life balance so heavily, as we want you to be happy while getting paid or enjoying scrolling through our catelog.

HORIZONS OVERVIEW

Horizons was created back in the fall of 2023 by Ernesto Guidos to bring forth his love and passion for both the world of art, and technology. Horizons is a community full of creative and bright minds, and bridges together the world of digital hand done art, and exciting new technologies revolving blockchain and NFTs.
