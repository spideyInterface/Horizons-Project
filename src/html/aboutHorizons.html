    What is Horizons?

    Horizons Basics

    I love art. Simple and straightforward. And I always have. I grew up in a very artistic and creative family with my mother and grandmother being the center of it all. I remember sitting beside my mother every week or so wathching her paint and draw, and just being fascinated by every stroke, how she would blend colors, what brushes she would use, the mess she would make and how her hands would be a colorful mess after she was done creating a beautiful painting after hours of work. I remember watching my grandmother make jewelry whenever we would go visit her on the weekends, and her showing me her collection of vinyl records and my grandfathers retro posters. I remember looking forward to my art classes while I was in school, and exceling in these classes and getting my teachers and classmates attention with the drawing I made, or the ceramic project I somehow managed to concoct. I remember always be so fascincated by color schemes, architectural designs, the shape and silouhettes of clothing, the interior design of rooms and buildings, the makeup people would wear, the jewelry I would see on wrists and necks, the code I would see a programmer develop, and the list goes on. I have always been a creative minded individual and nothing has changed as art and creativity is a part of who I am and how I go about life. It is not a conscious or pretentious decision, it is just how it is. But I am not the only one. I am a grain in the sahara desert. In this world, lies community and a sense of belonging and something that brings us together. That something is present amongst all walks of life whether natural or artificial, created or as is. And that is art.

    Why was Horizons created?

    Back in 2020, I was like many people in the world and was taken back by this new phenomenon known as Blockchain and Cryptocurrency. These two concepts and technologies completely took the workd by storm and was all over headlines, news, commercials, social media, financial outlets, campuses, offices, and it goes on and on. The buzz of the next new wave of technology was something hard to ignore, and along with my love for art, I always had a appreciation for good and useful technology, so I decided to delve in. And immediately, I was fascinated and hooked. I became extremely interested and invested in learning about Blockchain, what it is, how it works, what it can be used for etc and delved into many interesting concepts and projects(Ethereum being the big one). All of a sudden this concept of NFTs reached the surface and completely exploded in society, and once I found out what it was, I was like a kid in a candy store!

    See I absoutely love and adore digital art and animation. It has always been a passion of mine and its probably my favorite form of art there is. So when I found out that this new technology of NFTs can bridge the world between not only digital art, but art, assets and collectibles as a whole, my mind was blown. I went into a NFT rabbit hole and took it from there. Fast forward to the present, I began my journey as a Blockchain Developer and what better project for me to make than an NFT Marketplace? And here we are. Simply put, Horizons was created as an extension of my love for art and technology, and a way for me to showcase such to the world, whether big or small. And it's fun too...

    The core of it

    Horizons is an NFT marketplace that is built on top of the Sepolia Blokcchain Network, and is a place where users can mint, buy, sell, and explore various forms/types of NFTs. Horizons allows artists, investors, and creators to have creative and financial freedom, while giving its users control and authority over their own work or investments. Horizons welcomes users from all backgrounds and parts of the world, as well as welcoming expansion and growth. Horizons is not just meant to be a marketplace, but a community that users cannot wait to step into.

    Inspirations

    I did not just want to copy other NFT Marketplaces and create a application that looked and felt very similar to others within this industry. Like all forms of art, it is a personal telling of your personality and ideas that you possess, and the stories that follow them. By taking this approach, I created a application and Marketplace that is a extension of my personality and the layout of my creativity and well....my style!.

    Horizons takes inspiration from the opulence and richness of luxurious color palettes and designs, while advancing on contemporary and avant garde layouts with a animated atmosphere, while still embracing bright and vibrant colors. I wanted to make Horizons feel like your eyes were walking through a precious and beautiful art gallery in a animated and interactive digital world. Sharp designs with neutral tones, 3d transitions and animations, colorful and dark art displays and galleries etc while being tied and in tune with the end goal of Horizons, which is to allow creators and artists to explore the world of NFTs.

    No matter what background you come from, what art and colelctible style or category you enjoy, no matter where you are in the world, you can create and invest yourself within Horizons. No limits.

    With all this information you have read, I hope you received a good understanding as to what NFTs are and what they can potentially mean for artists, investors, and creators alike. This is an ever changing and evolving space as it is still a new technology and has so much growth potential. On top of that, the communities in this space are growing larger and more passionate about what is here and what is to come. So don't be afraid to jump on board and potentially get in early. If you are curious about some more subjects and topics about Blockchain, feel free to check out Horizons other articles! Thank you!
